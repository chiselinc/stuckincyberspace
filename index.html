stuck in cyberspace :: power in the information age

a master's thesis by chelsea palmer, 2015

Welcome to the barebones launch of my thesis website, wherein I begin to revisit html/css (gulp). For now, I think getting the material links up to my writing is more crucial than making a fancy pretty little site... after all, I kind of miss Web 1.0 when everything was quite boring and easy to parse.

The following are links to Google Docs of the various sections of my recent Master's Thesis. All inline citations within these documents can be traced to their sources within the (gigantic) bibliography document. Don't worry, I'll be cross-linking that for easier reading as soon as I can.


Feel free to contact me via email with any questions, comments, high-fives or condemnations. I'm looking forward to interdisciplinary collaboration and interstellar exploration as I move forward with both my theoretical and practical work.
